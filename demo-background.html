About IndoSys

Indian Symposium on Computer Systems

Computer systems research is critically important in this post-Moore era, where the traditional scaling of hardware performance is slowing down. The IndoSys symposium serves as a platform for researchers and practitioners in the field of computer systems to present their latest findings, share ideas, and discuss future directions. We invite high-quality submissions that provide novel insights and contribute to the advancement of computer systems.
